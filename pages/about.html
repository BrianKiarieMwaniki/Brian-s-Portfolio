My background

I was first introduced to programming back in 2011 by my late cousin, and I built a simple calculator with Visual Basic and from then on I knew that I wanted to become a software developer.

I have dabbled with a few programming languages and frameworks. In the recent past, I feel that I have settled more with the .NET Stack and Javascript. I am always learning and improving.

I this past year, I have had the priviledge to work on software development in a professional setting. I have worked with senior developers from whom I have learned a lot.

I enjoy solving problems programmatically and improving solutions. I care about writing readable code and organizing my work. I will quickly come up with a solution to a problem get something working as quick as I can, but I will always come back to the solution to improve on it, my main agendas being readability, performance and organization. There's is always room for improvement!

I'm excited to work on new projects, I see them as great opportunities to add on to what I already know and to learn something new.
