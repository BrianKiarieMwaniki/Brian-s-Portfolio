My background

I was first introduced to programming back in 2011 by my late cousin, ever since I have had a burning desire to become a software developer. The first program I wrote wasn't a Hello world application! , I wrote a simple addition calculator with Visual Basic -- it wasn't until I joined campus that I wrote my first hello world application.

I have dabbled with a few programming languages and frameworks, some of them being Java, C/C++, Dart, Spring framework, Flutter framework, native android development, PHP. Despite dropping out of uni, I haven't stopped learning.I am always learnign and improving.

After many years of trying, I landed my first job as a software developer. I got to work alongside senior developers, and from them I have gained invaluable experience.
